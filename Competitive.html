EDL: Competitive Soccer

There will tryouts from time to time (Assessments) of players to choose talented players (boys and girls). During the winter season the championship starts on the beginning of November and is played at the Louis Riel dome on Saturdays or Sundays. As for the summer the season it begins on May 4th at the Louis Riel dome and afterwards outside on different soccer fields
